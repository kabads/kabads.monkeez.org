Happy New Year Dave - you’ve got what your class wants

Sun Dec 30, 2012 by map[login:admin display_name:admin email:adam@monkeez.org first_name: last_name:]

Dave's latest message, http://cur.lv/e3wr which is frighteningly reassuring, gives us the message that 'we're doing the right thing' - this is a global race, that we must keep up with. However, you need to delve behind what he's saying. Basically, this is a global race and we need to keep up with other countries. These countries being China and India. However, our economies are completely different from these manufacturing countries.
