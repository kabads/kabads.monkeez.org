A good article on why e-books are being locked down just to keep the paper industry in business. monki.biz/1r
  • March 16, 2012
http://monki.biz/1r Publishing is a tough industry to be in, no doubt about it. Things are rapidly changing and market forces are being swayed beyond the traditional publisher's influence. Things are now so easy to share and distribute, that these old gate-keepers can no longer steer what people are looking at. However, e-books are being subjected to this, so that instead of paper being pushed around and controlled,  instead digital files are being controlled and again, authors are being expected to go through the publisher, instead of dealing directly with their audience.
